Strong Password Audit

A good day starts with strong passwords

>_ What to expect

Security begins with a strong password. We analyze the quality and strength of the passwords used by your employees and systems. With robust authentication, you will reduce the risk of unauthorized access and protect your digital assets from unwanted intrusions.

Through a comprehensive password cracking activity, we can provide you with a complete statistical overview of the composition of passwords used by users in your company and answer the following questions:


  • Do all passwords meet the minimum complexity policies?
  • Are some user groups more exposed than others?
  • How many of my users' passwords are secure?
  • Are known compromised passwords in use?
  • Are there trivial or easily predictable patterns?
  • What are the top 10 most common passwords in the company?
