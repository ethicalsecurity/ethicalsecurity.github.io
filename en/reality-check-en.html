PROCESS How does Reality Check work?
STEP 1 PRELIMINARY ANALYSIS
We study your network and critical systems to identify the main weak points.
STEP 2 TARGETED TEST
We send a probe into the network, which can be software or an application, with which we will perform a targeted attack on the most vulnerable components
STEP 3 FINAL REPORT
You'll receive a report with the results, identified risks, and advice to improve your security. If you wish, we'll evaluate an intervention to resolve the issues
